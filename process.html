Common Questions

Everything You
Want to Know

Will the finished space actually match the 3D render?

Yes — this is our core promise. Every material, colour, and fixture shown in the 3D render is the exact item specified for your project. We have delivered over 150 projects with a 100% match rate between the approved design and the final execution.

How long does a typical project take?

A modular kitchen typically takes 3–4 weeks from approval to handover. A 2BHK complete interior takes 6–8 weeks. Commercial office projects are phased to minimize business disruption. We commit to a date upfront — and we keep it.

Can I make changes after the 3D design is approved?

Minor changes before material procurement begins can be accommodated. However, once materials are ordered and work begins, structural changes may affect the timeline and cost. This is why we invest so heavily in the design approval phase — we want you to love it before we build it.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Absolutely. We have delivered 60+ commercial projects including offices, retail showrooms, and hospitality spaces — and 90+ residential projects including modular kitchens, full-home interiors, and bedroom suites across Vimannagar, Vishrantwadi, Kalyani Nagar, and greater Pune.

What is included in the free site visit?

Our designer visits your space, takes precise measurements, discusses your lifestyle, aesthetic preferences, and budget in detail. You'll also receive an initial style direction moodboard at no cost. There is absolutely no obligation to proceed.

Do you provide a warranty on your work?

Yes. All Ozean Groups projects come with a 12-month warranty on workmanship. If any snag or issue arises due to our execution, we resolve it within 48 hours at no additional cost to you.
